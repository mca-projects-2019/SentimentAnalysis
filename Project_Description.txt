Analysis of Top Colleges

*Sentiment Analysis is the classification of the polarity of a given text in the document,sentence or phrase.
 The goal is to determine whether the expressed opinion in the text is positive , negative or neutral.

*Social Media has captured the attention of the entire world as it is thundering fast in sending thoughts across the globe,
 user friendly and free of cost requiring only a working internet connection.Twitter is one such well known micro-blogging site,
 existing system perform sentiment analysis of people’s opinions regarding top colleges in India.
 Existing system used Naïve Bayes,SVM and ANN  for finding top colleges from the twitter data.
 In proposed System various Algorithms such as SVM,CNN,KNN,Decision Tree  etc will be used and compare the efficiency of various algorithms.
